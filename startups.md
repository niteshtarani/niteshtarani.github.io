			<h2>YellowBus</h2>
		</header>
		<p>Co-founded a comprehensive school transportation safety platform that revolutionizes how schools manage student commutes and communicate with parents. YellowBus addresses critical safety concerns while streamlining administrative logistics for educational institutions.</p>
		
		<h3>Key Features & Impact</h3>
		<ul>
			<li><strong>Real-Time GPS Tracking:</strong> Live bus location monitoring across all routes, providing parents and administrators with accurate, up-to-the-minute visibility</li>
			<li><strong>Automated Safety Alerts:</strong> Instant notifications for student boarding and deboarding events, ensuring accountability and giving parents peace of mind</li>
			<li><strong>Integrated Communication Hub:</strong> Centralized platform for school-wide announcements and event updates, keeping parents engaged with their child's educational community</li>
		</ul>
		<p><em>Impact:</em> Enhanced student safety, reduced parent anxiety, and improved operational efficiency for school transportation departments.</p>

			<li><strong>Smart Reservations:</strong> Streamlined booking system eliminates friction in the reservation process</li>
			<li><strong>Exclusive Offers:</strong> In-app coupons and dining discounts provide value to users while driving traffic to partner venues</li>
		</ul>
		<p><em>Value Proposition:</em> Creating a vibrant ecosystem that enhances social experiences while helping restaurants and venues fill seats through intelligent networking.</p>
